MotoMachi Data Deletion Request

MotoMachi allows users to delete their data and account at any time.

How to Delete Your Data

  • Remove individual logs or records inside the app
  • Delete your user profile through the app settings
  • Request account deletion by contacting us via email

What Happens After Deletion

When a user profile is deleted, all associated account information and cloud-stored data including vehicle logs, ride journals, fuel entries, and maintenance records are permanently removed.

This action cannot be undone.
